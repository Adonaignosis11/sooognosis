Other Filetypes

A variety of other file formats are supported through the use of medcon/libmdc, including: Acr/Nema 2.0, Analyze (SPM), InterFile3.3, and Gif87a/89a. For more current information, check out the xmedcon/libmdc webpage at: http://xmedcon.sourceforge.net
