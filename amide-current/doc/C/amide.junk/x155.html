Exporting a View

To export one of the views (transverse/coronal/sagittal) to an external image file, select "File->Export View->[view]" from the menu. The saved data format is determined by the extension of the filename. A large variety of image data formats can be saved, including JPEG [.jpg], TIFF [.tiff], Postscript [.ps], Encapsulated Postscript [.eps], and PNG [.png].
